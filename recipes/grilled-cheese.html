Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Buttah
  • Herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Spread butter on both sides of the bread.
  2. Heat up a pan.
  3. Throw some cheese on the bread and place in the frying pan.
  4. Cook until bread is golden brown.
